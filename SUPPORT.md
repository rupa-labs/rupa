# Support Guide 🤝

Thank you for using Rupaui! If you need help, have questions, or want to discuss the framework, please use the following channels.

---

## 🙋 Getting Help

1.  **Documentation:** Most answers can be found in our comprehensive [9-Layer Blueprint](./docs/README.md).
2.  **GitHub Issues:** Use issues for bug reports and feature requests. Please search existing issues before opening a new one.
3.  **Discussions:** (Planned) We will soon open GitHub Discussions for general Q&A and architectural brainstorming.

---

## 🛠️ Reporting a Bug

When reporting a bug, please provide:
- A clear description of the behavior.
- Your Operating System and GPU (if GUI-related).
- A minimal reproducible example (code snippet).

---

## 📧 Private Contact

For security-related issues or private inquiries, please contact the maintainers at **reasnov.app@gmail.com**.

---

*Note: As Rupaui is currently in its early stages (v0), response times may vary. We appreciate your patience!*
